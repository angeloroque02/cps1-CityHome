Sky Home

A joint venture between City Home and Triple T Properties, two of the country’s leading property developers, Sky Home is City Home first master-planned mixed-used development in Quezon City and Pasig. Covering 30 hectares, it is envisioned to be the greenest urban estate in Metro Manila and the largest along the C-5 corridor.  

Cyber City

Complementing the flexibility of Cyber City, City Home presents its latest addition to High Street South’s esteemed selection of residential towers. A 105-storey vertical community unveiling a curated selection of timeless spaces. An address of classic sensibility.
